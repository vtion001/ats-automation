Available Automations

This document describes all automations available in the ATS system. Automations are organized by client and priority.

Flyland Recovery

1. CTM-SF Auto Access HIGH

Description: Automatically opens Salesforce account when a call comes via CTM.

  • Trigger: Incoming call detected in CTM
  • Action: Opens Salesforce search with caller's phone number
  • Time Saved: 1-3 minutes per call
2. Auto Note Generator HIGH

Description: Automatically generates notes for unqualified or duplicate calls.

  • Trigger: Disposition set to "unqualified" or "duplicate"
  • Action: Generates pre-formatted note based on disposition
  • Time Saved: 1-2 minutes per call
3. Chat Reply Suggester MEDIUM

Description: Suggests replies for chat conversations based on context.

  • Trigger: Customer message in ZOHO chat
  • Action: Shows 2-3 suggested replies in overlay
  • Time Saved: 1-3 minutes per chat
4. Wrap-Up Sync MEDIUM

Description: Auto-syncs disposition data to all relevant systems.

  • Trigger: Call wrap-up completed
  • Action: Updates CTM, Salesforce, and tracker simultaneously
  • Time Saved: Eliminates duplicate entry

Legacy

1. Wrap-Up Auto-Sync HIGH

Description: Post-call, auto-sync disposition data to all relevant systems.

2. Customer History Auto-Pull HIGH

Description: Auto-load caller history and remind agent of missed details.

3. Form-Filler & AI Queue Prioritizer MEDIUM

Description: DOM-based form automation with AI prioritization.

TBT (We The Best)

1. Auto-Lead Pruning HIGH

Description: Automatically remove unresponsive leads from the queue.

2. Auto-Lead Pull & Inbound Routing HIGH

Description: Auto-detect and route incoming leads from Salesforce queue.

3. Text Reply Suggester MEDIUM

Description: Template-based responses for outbound SMS/text.

Banyan

1. CTM-SF Auto-Account Pop-Up HIGH

Description: Auto-populate Salesforce data when call arrives via CTM.

2. Auto-Call Tracking & Form/Note Auto-Fill HIGH

Description: After call, auto-update Google Form tracker AND Salesforce.

Takahami

1. Auto-Fax for Appeals HIGH

Description: Auto-submit fax appeals for claims.

2. Claim-Type-Based Appeal Router HIGH

Description: Auto-determine appeal destination based on claim type.

Element Medical Billing

1. PDF Auto-Filler HIGH

Description: Auto-fill PDF forms with patient data from open portal.

2. Insurance Portal Auto-Lookup HIGH

Description: Automatically check claim status in insurance portals.
